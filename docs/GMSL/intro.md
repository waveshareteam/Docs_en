# About GMSL

GMSL (Gigabit Multimedia Serial Link) is a high-speed serial transmission technology specifically designed for transmitting high-bandwidth multimedia data, featuring long-distance transmission, low latency, high anti-interference performance and simple wiring. Primarily applied in automotive electronics and industrial applications, GMSL technology is capable of transmitting high-definition video, audio and data signals over a single cable. It enables long-distance signal transmission via coaxial cables or twisted pairs.
